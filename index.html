About Us

AISRI DIRECT is based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. We do direct shipping from suppliers to customers using UPI-based checkout and SKU codes. Every order is for one product at a time, so tracking and support stays simple and clear.

Products

Admin – Manage Products

All products shown on the Home page come from products.json. Edit that file in your GitHub repo to add, remove, or update products. 

{
  "id": "1000",
  "name": "Sample Item",
  "price": 199,
  "category": "bottles",
  "statusText": "In Stock",
  "description": "Sample description",
  "image": ""
}

After editing products.json, commit and push to GitHub. Netlify will automatically redeploy your site with the updated products.
